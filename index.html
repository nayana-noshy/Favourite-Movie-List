Mollywood Gems

Chithram

CHITHRAM (1988) is a Malayalam comedy-drama directed by Priyadarshan, starring Mohanlal. The film follows Vishnu, who is hired to pose as Kalyani's husband to fulfill her father’s last wish. As secrets unfold, emotions grow complicated. Known for its blend of humor, romance, and tragedy, Chithram became a massive hit and remains a timeless classic in Malayalam cinema.

Kannur Squad

KANNUR SQUAD (2023) is a Malayalam action-thriller starring Mammootty as a retired police officer who returns to solve a critical case in the politically charged Kannur region. The film follows his efforts to fight corruption and violence with a special squad.

Malootty

MALOOTTY (1990) is a Malayalam drama about a 5-year-old girl who gets trapped in a drill hole during a vacation. Jayaram plays her father, who struggles to rescue her. Shamili won the Kerala State Film Award for Best Child Artist for her role.

Top English Picks

Predestination

PREDESTINATION (2014) is a time-travel thriller where an agent (Ethan Hawke) recruits John (Sarah Snook) to stop a bomber. As the story unfolds, it’s revealed that John, Jane, the agent, and the bomber are all the same person, trapped in a predestination paradox. The film explores themes of identity and fate.

Memento

MEMENTO (2000) is a thriller about Leonard (Guy Pearce), a man with short-term memory loss, seeking revenge for his wife’s murder. Using tattoos and photos to track clues, the story unfolds in reverse order, creating a suspenseful and disorienting narrative.

Interstellar

INTERSTELLAR (2014) is a sci-fi film about astronauts led by Cooper (Matthew McConaughey) who travel through a wormhole to find a new planet for humanity as Earth faces collapse. It explores love, time, and survival.

Bollywood Treasures

Kal Ho Naa Ho

KAL HO NAA HO (2003) is a Bollywood romantic drama starring Shah Rukh Khan, Preity Zinta, and Saif Ali Khan. It tells the story of Naina, whose life changes when she meets her lively neighbor Aman. Hiding a tragic secret, Aman helps her find love with her best friend, Rohit. Known for its emotions and music, it’s a Bollywood classic.

Taare Zameen Par

TAARE ZAMEEN PAR (2007) is a Bollywood drama about Ishaan, a boy with dyslexia, struggling in school. His teacher, Ram, helps him discover his talents, showing the importance of understanding every child's uniqueness.

KHABI KHUSHI KHABIE GHAM (2001) is a Bollywood family drama starring Amitabh Bachchan, Shah Rukh Khan, Kajol, and Hrithik Roshan. It follows a wealthy family facing struggles when their son marries against their wishes, exploring love, relationships, and family bonds.
